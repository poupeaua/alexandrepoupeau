About

This site would not be entirely a description of myself if there was no part dedicated to ecology. Really I am preoccupated about the subject since I am young. When I was 18, I remember saying to a friend of mine : "If you do not work for the environment during your lifetime, it is not worth working at all."

I began to start studying the problematic of our century later. The book that completely changed the way I conceive this subject is "This changes everything" written by Naomi Klein. Feel free to share this page to the people you know.
